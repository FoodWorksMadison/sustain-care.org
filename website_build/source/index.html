Sustain Care

a community response to the COVID-19 crisis

Sustain Care aims to collect and promote all community care initiatives in and around Madison that put healthy prepared meals in the hands of essential workers, caregivers and their families, or any others experiencing food insecurity.

Just getting started...

We want to support our local food economy — our farmers, producers, distributors, restaurateurs, cooks and other food-service workers. We also aim to make sure that everyone has both safe working conditions with all currently advised public health protocols and is paid a fair wage for their work.

This website is brand new and has a lot of features missing, but we wanted to get the support flowing ASAP.

Please browse our curated list of community care initiatives, choose one that resonates with you, and give directly and generously if you can.
