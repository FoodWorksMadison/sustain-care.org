How to Donate:

These big buttons will take you to PayPal, where you can choose an amount and whether you want your donation to be recurring.

You can always donate directly by choosing a project that resononates with you. But if you can't choose, or would like us to choose for you, you can give a one time donation or better yet, a recurring donation with the buttons here.

If you donate here, 100% of your donation will go to the projects/workers (less paypal fees). We will fairly direct the funds to the projects with the most effectiveness who address the most acute need at the time.

How Much?

Don't know how much is appropriate? If you can, consider generously donating what you'd normally spend going out to eat during the week. Or, maybe what you might tip during the week. Or whatever you can. We know that things are tight all around now. Get in touch if you want to give a large amount and are worried about the fees.

Sustain Care is affiliated with FoodWorks Madison, a tax-exempt, registered 501(c)(3) charitable organization (EIN:81-4562690). Donations to Sustain Care will be made and distributed through FoodWorks, and are tax-deductible to the fullest extent of the law.
