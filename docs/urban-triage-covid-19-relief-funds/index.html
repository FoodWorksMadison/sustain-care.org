Urban Triage COVID-19 Relief Funds

[LOGO] [PHOTO(S)]

We are partnering with Ribmasters WI to provide FREE home-cooked meals to 100 people for 10 weeks. WE would like to expand the number of families to 200 and increase the length of time we offer meals. In addition to increasing the number of resources, we provide families and individuals who are the most vulnerable.

As part of our campaign, we are providing pantry/grocery delivery services to 50 families a week in partnership with N Motion Non-Medical Transportation, Mt. Zion Baptist Church, Sherman Ave United Methodist Church and SS Morris Community AME Church
