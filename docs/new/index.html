Information You'll Need:

You can always change it later, but for starters, gather these bits:

  • Your Project's name, description and web link (if you have one)
  • Basic info about you (or your chosen representative), and how you want to be contacted
  • How people can donate funds (a link)
  • Images like a logo or photo if you have them
  • Information about organizations or institutions that back your initiative (if you have some)
