Initiativo de Granjas a Familias / Farms to Families Initiative

Farms to Families/Fondo de Granjas a Familias is an emergency initiative created by a partnership between REAP and Roots4Change. The program will purchase fresh, healthy, locally grown food from regional family farms and provide it to Madison-area Latino/Indigenous residents and families hard-hit by job loss, food insecurity, lack of access to social services, and federal anti-immigration provisions.
